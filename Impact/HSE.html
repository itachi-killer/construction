Health, Safety and Environment (HSE)

We are committed to the health, safety and wellbeing of our workforce as well as environment protection through responsible consumption of resources and prevention of pollution.

This means, with an eye on Sustainable Development Goals, achieving excellence in ESG (Environmental, Social and Governance) aspects related to our business is the way forward for us.

Innovative and path-breaking programs

Many programs and initiatives were devised and implemented to establish a strong HSE culture across the organization.

ईश्वरESWER
Either Safe Work Else Refusal

Our motto to demonstrate zero
tolerance toward lack of safety.

प्राणPRAN
Prevent Repetition of Accidents Necessarily

A monthly alert to learn from mistakes and
prevent any recurrence.

रक्षाRACSHA
Remote Audit, Confirmation and
Simulated HSE inspection Agenda

A comprehensive online audit module.

रोमेशROMESH
Respect, Own and Manage Environment,
Safety and Health

An approach adopted by the employees
towards HSE.

Incorporating HSE considerations in business decisions and operations

Adopting preventive measures in business processes and practices

Setting short-term and long-term goals towards optimal HSE standards

Making sure worker colonies, offices, and sites are safe, sustainable, and comfortable

Working with a Top-Down Approach where safety at a project site is the responsibility of the Project Head

Acknowledging ‘Safety is Priority’ well across the organization

Empowering the HSE department by giving a ‘Governance’ status

Promoting awareness among employees, contractors, business partners and other stakeholders to develop a positive HSE culture

Providing a feedback mechanism and constant engagement with all stakeholders

Encouraging all stakeholders to contribute towards HSE through a Monthly Reward program

We are a diversified business conglomerate with clients in over 50 countries, delivering challenging projects for over 150 years. We operate in 6 major business areas with 13 group companies, 3 divisions and a strong workforce of over 60,000 people from across 40+ nationalities.

Shapoorji Pallonji And Company Private Limited established in 1865 in India is a global, diversified institution with a leading presence in the sectors of Engineering & Construction, Infrastructure, Real Estate, Water, Energy and Financial Services. A strong 35,000 people employee base delivers end-to-end solutions across 40 nations. We build megastructures, develop multifaceted iconic landmarks, drive innovative technologies in water management, renewable energy, oil & gas and power, with a focus on good governance and sustainable development, to engineer a better planet.
